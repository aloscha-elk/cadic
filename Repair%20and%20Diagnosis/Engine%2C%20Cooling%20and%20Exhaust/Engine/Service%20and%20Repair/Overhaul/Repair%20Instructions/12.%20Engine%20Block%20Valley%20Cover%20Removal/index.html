12. Engine Block Valley Cover Removal



Engine Valley Cover Removal




1. Remove the valley cover bolts (506).
2. Remove the valley cover (555) and gasket (556).
3. Remove the O-ring seals (537) from the cover.




4. Remove the oil pressure sensor (706) and washer (707), as required.
