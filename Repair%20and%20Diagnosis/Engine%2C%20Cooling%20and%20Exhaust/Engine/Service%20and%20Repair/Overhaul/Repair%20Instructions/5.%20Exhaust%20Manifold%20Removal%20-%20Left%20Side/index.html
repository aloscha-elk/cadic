5. Exhaust Manifold Removal - Left Side



Exhaust Manifold Removal - Left Side




1. Remove the spark plug wires from the spark plugs.
2. Remove the exhaust manifold, bolts, and gasket.
3. Discard the gasket.




4. Remove the heat shield (603) and bolts (604) from the manifold (600), as required.
5. Remove the studs (605), as required.
