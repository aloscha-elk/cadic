Forward Clutch Piston Travel Check



Forward Clutch Piston Travel Check




1. Use feeler gauges to check the forward clutch plate travel. Check travel between the forward clutch backing plate retainer ring (651) and the forward clutch selective backing plate (650).
The forward clutch plate travel should be:

0.876-1.866 mm (0.034-0.073 in)

2. Select the proper forward clutch selective backing plate (650) to obtain the correct travel. Refer to Forward Clutch Backing Plate Selection.
