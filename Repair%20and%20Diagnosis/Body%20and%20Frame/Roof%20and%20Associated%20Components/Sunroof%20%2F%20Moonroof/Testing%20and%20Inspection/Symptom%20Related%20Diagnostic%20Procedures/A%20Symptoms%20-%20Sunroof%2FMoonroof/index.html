A Symptoms - Sunroof/Moonroof



SYMPTOMS - SUNROOF/MOONROOF

IMPORTANT: Review the system description in order to familiarize yourself with the system functions. Refer to Sunroof Description and Operation.

Visual/Physical Inspection
- Inspect for aftermarket devices which could affect the operation of the Sunroof System.
- Inspect the easily accessible or visible system components for obvious damage or conditions which could cause the symptom.

Intermittent
Faulty electrical connections or wiring may be the cause of intermittent conditions.

Symptom List
Refer to Power Sunroof Inoperative in order to diagnose the symptom.
