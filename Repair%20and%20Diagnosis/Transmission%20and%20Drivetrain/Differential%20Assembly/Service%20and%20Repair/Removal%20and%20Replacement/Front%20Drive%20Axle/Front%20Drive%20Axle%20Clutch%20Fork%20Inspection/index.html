Front Drive Axle Clutch Fork Inspection



Front Drive Axle Clutch Fork Inspection

^ Inspect the carrier connector for damaged splines and teeth. Replace as required.
^ Inspect the shift fork for wear, scoring, and damage to the thrust surfaces. Replace as required.
^ Inspect the differential sleeve and the inner output shaft for damaged splines and teeth. Replace as required.
^ Inspect the damper spring for breakage. Replace or repair the damper spring as needed.
^ Inspect the differential actuator and the engagement switch for damage and frayed wiring.
