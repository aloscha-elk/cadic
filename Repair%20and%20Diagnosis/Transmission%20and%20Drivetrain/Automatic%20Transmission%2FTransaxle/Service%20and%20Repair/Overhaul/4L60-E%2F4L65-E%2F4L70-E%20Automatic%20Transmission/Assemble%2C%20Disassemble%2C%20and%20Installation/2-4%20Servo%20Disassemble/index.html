2-4 Servo Disassemble



2-4 Servo Disassemble

Tools Required

J 22269-01 Accumulator and Servo Piston Remover




1. Use a J 22269-01 to compress the second apply piston assembly (17).
2. Remove the second apply piston retaining ring (14).
3. Remove the servo cushion spring retainer (15), the servo cushion outer spring (16) and the servo cushion inner spring (105) (model dependent).
