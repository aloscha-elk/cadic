Transmission Fluid Pan Installation



Transmission Fluid Pan Installation





Important: You may reuse the transmission oil pan gasket (29).

1. Install the transmission oil pan gasket (29).
2. Install the transmission oil pan magnet (30) into the transmission oil pan (28).

Notice: Position the wiring harness properly when installing the transmission oil pan or it may be crushed between the valve body and the oil pan.

3. Install the transmission oil pan (28).

Notice: Refer to Fastener Notice.

4. Install the transmission oil pan bolts (27).

Tighten the bolts to 24 N.m (18 lb ft).
