Power Brake Booster Inlet Hose Replacement



Power Brake Booster Inlet Hose Replacement

Removal Procedure





Notice: Refer to Power Steering Hose Disconnected Notice.

1. Raise the vehicle. Refer to Lifting and Jacking the Vehicle.
2. Remove the engine protection shield.
3. Install a drain pan under the vehicle.
4. Remove the brake booster inlet hose from the power steering pump (3).
5. Lower the vehicle.
6. Remove the brake booster inlet hose (1) from the brake booster (2).
7. Remove the brake booster inlet hose from the vehicle.

Installation Procedure





Notice: Refer to Installing Hoses Without Twists or Bends Notice.

1. Route the hose in the same position the hose occupied prior to removal.
2. Install the brake booster inlet hose (1) to the brake booster (2). Hand tighten only.
3. Raise the vehicle.

Notice: Refer to Fastener Notice.

4. Install the brake booster inlet hose to the power steering pump (3).

Tighten the brake booster inlet hose fitting at the power steering pump to 32 N.m (24 lb ft).

5. Install the engine protection shield.
6. Lower the vehicle.
7. Tighten the brake booster inlet hose to brake booster.

Tighten the brake booster inlet hose fitting to 32 N.m (24 lb ft).

8. Remove the drain pan from under the vehicle.
9. Bleed the power steering system. Refer to Power Steering System Bleeding.
