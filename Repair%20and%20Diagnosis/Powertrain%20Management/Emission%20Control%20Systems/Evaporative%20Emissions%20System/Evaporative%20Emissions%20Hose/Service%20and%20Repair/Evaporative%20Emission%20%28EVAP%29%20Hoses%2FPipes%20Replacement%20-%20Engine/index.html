Evaporative Emission (EVAP) Hoses/Pipes Replacement - Engine



EVAPORATIVE EMISSION HOSES/PIPES REPLACEMENT - ENGINE

REMOVAL PROCEDURE

IMPORTANT: Clean all evaporative emission (EVAP) line connections and surrounding areas prior to disconnecting the fittings in order to avoid possible system contamination.




1. Remove the air cleaner outlet duct.
2. Disconnect the EVAP line quick connect fitting at the intake manifold.
3. Disconnect the EVAP line quick connect fitting at the EVAP canister purge solenoid.

INSTALLATION PROCEDURE




1. Connect the EVAP line quick connect fitting at the EVAP canister purge solenoid.
2. Connect the EVAP line quick connect fitting at the intake manifold.
3. Install the air cleaner outlet duct.
