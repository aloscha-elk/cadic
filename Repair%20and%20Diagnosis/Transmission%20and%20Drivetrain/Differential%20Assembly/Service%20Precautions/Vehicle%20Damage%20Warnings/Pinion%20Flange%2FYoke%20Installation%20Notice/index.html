Pinion Flange/Yoke Installation Notice



Pinion Flange/Yoke Installation Notice

Notice: Do not hammer the pinion flange/yoke onto the pinion shaft. Pinion components may be damaged if the pinion flange/yoke is hammered onto the pinion shaft.
