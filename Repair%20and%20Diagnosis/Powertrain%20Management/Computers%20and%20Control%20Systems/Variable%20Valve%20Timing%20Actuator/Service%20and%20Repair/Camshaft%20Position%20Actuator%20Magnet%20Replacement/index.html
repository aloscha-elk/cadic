Camshaft Position Actuator Magnet Replacement



Camshaft Position Actuator Magnet Replacement

Removal Procedure




1. Remove the water pump.
2. Disconnect the engine harness electrical connector from the camshaft position (CMP) actuator magnet.
3. Remove the CMP actuator magnet bolts (751) and magnet (752).
4. Remove and discard the CMP actuator magnet gasket (753).

Installation Procedure

Important: The gasket surface should be free of oil or other foreign material during assembly.




1. Install a NEW CMP actuator magnet gasket (753) onto the CMP actuator magnet.
2. Install the CMP actuator magnet (752) to the front cover.

Notice: Refer to Fastener Notice.

3. Install the CMP actuator magnet bolts (751).

Tighten the bolts to 12 N.m (106 lb in).

4. Connect the engine harness electrical connector to the CMP actuator magnet.
5. Install the water pump.
