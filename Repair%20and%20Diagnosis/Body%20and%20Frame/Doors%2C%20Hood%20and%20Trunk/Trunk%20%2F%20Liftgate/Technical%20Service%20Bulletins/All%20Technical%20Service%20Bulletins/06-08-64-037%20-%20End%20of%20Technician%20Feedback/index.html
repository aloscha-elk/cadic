06-08-64-037 - End of Technician Feedback

ENGINEERING INFORMATION

Bulletin No.: 06-08-64-037A

Date: May 07, 2008

Subject:
EI06034 - Liftgate, Door Handle Broke Broken, Lock, Won't Open, Inoperative

Models:
2007 Cadillac Escalade, Escalade ESV, Escalade EXT
2007 Chevrolet Avalanche, Suburban, Tahoe
2007 GMC Yukon, Yukon XL, Yukon Denali, Yukon Denali XL

Supercede:

This bulletin is being revised to end the need for technician feedback. Please discard Corporate Bulletin Number (Section 08 - Body and Accessories).

Based on technician feedback, engineering has made a tool change for the liftgate handle to prevent the bosses from breaking.
