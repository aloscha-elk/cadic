Wheels/Tires - Plus-Size Wheel and Tire Systems

Bulletin No.: 06-03-10-005

Date: May 25, 2006

INFORMATION

Subject:
Plus-Size Wheel and Tire Systems for GM Vehicles

Models:
2007 Cadillac Escalade, Escalade ESV, Escalade EXT
2007 Chevrolet Avalanche, Suburban, Tahoe
2007 GMC Yukon, Yukon XL, Yukon Denali, Yukon Denali XL

The GM Accessories 22" wheel and TPC tire system has been validated for use on 2007 Cadillac Escalade, Escalade ESV, Escalade EXT, GMC Yukon Denali and Yukon Denali XL only. Other new-style large utility vehicles such as the 2007 Avalanche, Tahoe, Yukon, Suburban and Yukon XL are NOT approved applications for the 22" wheel and TPC tire systems.

These GM approved applications include the unique control system calibrations for each vehicle, including anti-lock brake, electronic vehicle stability enhancement and speedometer calibrations.

GM strongly recommends AGAINST replacement of original equipment wheel and tire systems with other systems that use non-GM wheels or any wheels (including GM Accessories wheels) larger than specified by GM in approved applications, due to the following:

^ Wheel and tire systems are an integral part of a vehicle safety and performance system. Each GM Accessories wheel and TPC tire system is designed and released for the specified vehicle applications only.

^ Correct calibrations are required to maintain the intended performance of the vehicle systems.

^ Without proper integration of the wheel and tire system on a specific vehicle, larger wheel and tire systems may deliver:

- Decreased brake system performance

- Compromised vehicle handling and rollover resistance

- Changes in steering system performance

- Increased loads and stresses to the suspension, frame, and body that may decrease the life of vehicle components and systems

- Decreased ride performance

- Compromised operations of anti-lock brakes, electronic stability control and tire pressure monitoring systems

Plus size wheel and tire systems may even cause changes in occupant protection or brake system performance that, as a result, may cause the vehicle to fail to meet Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standards. For all these reasons, General Motors strongly recommends AGAINST the addition of plus-size wheel and tire systems on any vehicles for which these systems were not validated.

Additionally, non-approved installations or alterations to the original equipment vehicles as distributed by General Motors are not covered by the General Motors New Vehicle Limited Warranty. This warranty does not cover any damage or failure resulting from modification or alteration to the vehicle's original equipment as manufactured or assembled by General Motors except for dealer installed parts or accessories such as Delco radio or trailering hitch package, etc.
