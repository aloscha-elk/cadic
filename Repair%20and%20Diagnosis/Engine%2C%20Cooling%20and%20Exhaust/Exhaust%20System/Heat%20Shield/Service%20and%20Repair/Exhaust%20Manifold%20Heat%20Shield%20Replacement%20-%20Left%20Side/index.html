Exhaust Manifold Heat Shield Replacement - Left Side



Exhaust Manifold Heat Shield Replacement - Left Side

Removal Procedure




1. Remove the spark plug wires. Refer to Spark Plug Replacement.
2. Remove the exhaust manifold heat shield bolts and heat shield from the exhaust manifold.

Installation Procedure





Notice: Refer to Fastener Notice.

1. Position the exhaust manifold heat shield to the exhaust manifold and install the heat shield bolts.

Tighten the bolts to 9 N.m (80 lb in).

2. Install the spark plug wires. Refer to Spark Plug Replacement.
