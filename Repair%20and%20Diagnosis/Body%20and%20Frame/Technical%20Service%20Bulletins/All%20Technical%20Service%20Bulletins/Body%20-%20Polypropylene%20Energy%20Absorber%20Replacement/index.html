Body - Polypropylene Energy Absorber Replacement

Bulletin No.: 07-08-63-001

Date: April 17, 2007

INFORMATION

Subject:
Information on Repair of Polypropylene Energy Absorbers

Models:
2007 and Prior GM Passenger Cars and Trucks (including Saturn)
2007 and Prior HUMMER H2, H3
2005-2007 Saab 9-7X

Supercede:

This bulletin is being revised to change the repair information. Please discard Corporate Bulletin Number 63-20-02 (Section 8 - Body and Accessories).

Because the energy absorbers are relatively low in cost to replace, it is now more cost efficient to replace the energy absorbers whenever they are damaged.
