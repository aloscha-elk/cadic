58. Engine Block Valley Cover Cleaning and Inspection



Engine Valley Cover Cleaning and Inspection




1. Clean the valley cover in solvent.

Caution: Refer to Safety Glasses Caution.

2. Dry with compressed air.
3. Inspect for damaged threads (1) and damaged sealing surfaces (2).
